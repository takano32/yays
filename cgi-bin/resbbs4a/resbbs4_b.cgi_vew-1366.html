　【いま気になる小説は、】

    * TG News: Anime TV series "Tenshi na Konamaiki" coming soon in Japan. A young boy named Megumi Amatsuka picks up an old book one day. There was a demon living in the book and he says that he can grant one's wish. Megumi alwas wanted to be a tough man so he makes his wish. Unfortunately the demon was a wicked one and he magically turns Megumi into a cute looking girl. Megumi grows up as a girl, boyish but pretty girl, and now she is going to be a highschool student. The story starts from here... Unlike Ranma1/2, TG amusement is not the main part of the story although Megumi's character is very charming. 'Tenshi na Konamaiki' can be translated as 'A Cheeky Angel.'
    Original comic series running in Weekly Shonen-Sunday, comic artist: Hiroyuki Nishimori. Comic book "Tenshi na Konamaiki"(issue 1-11) is published by Shogakukan.
