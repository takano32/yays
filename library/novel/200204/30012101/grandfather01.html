Am I a grandfather!?
The 1st story

The author : Yui Satou

　

This is the mysterious story that occurred with a certain apartment house.



A grandfather came to the celebration of Yumiko that graduated from an elementary school.

Now the grandfather is relaxing with a sofa.

Yumiko and grandfather are sitting down to the sofa.

"Thank you for the grandfather. Thank you for the present of the grandfather, personal computer."

Yumiko was happy to while touching the first latest 21st century personal computer that was presented from the grandfather.

"I think that it is usually."

A grandchild looked joyful.

The initial setting of the personal computer ended.

Yumiko began to make the cookies to the gratitude.

"As for the papa and mother are they that are going out?"

Yumiko was making the cookies a round form.

"The papa is a job and the mother travels. Two return home in the evening."

The grandfather who was drinking tea said.

"I come back after I eat dinner."

A grandfather lived in distantly.

A grandfather lived in the place where is able to come only several times in the month.

Yumiko was pleased that a grandfather eats together.

The cookies were completed.

The cookies served it on the plate that I was warming a little.

Yumiko had the cookies and walked toward the sofa.

The grandfather was pleased.

There were the time, big earthquake.

The fire extinguisher falls and a room became pure white.

The earthquake is terrible.

"Did the fire extinguisher fall?"

Yumiko who it was wrapped to the foam of the fire extinguisher got confused.

Yumiko wiped the face. The girl of the same figure as the self was sitting down, in the before of Yumiko. Yumiko was surprised.

Yumiko cried out.

"There is the same girl as me!"

Taking out the voice Yumiko felt sense of incongruity.

"I seem to have become a grandfather. "

A grandfather who was hearing the voice said,

"Yumiko? "

The grandfather who became young asked it with the voice of Yumiko.

"The body of my body and, grandfather were exchanged. "

Yumiko talks while worrying. Anyway the body was exchanged.

"I have no way but to live with this body for a while. "

"I was looking forward to junior high school life. It is disagreeable to live with the body of a grandfather. "

Yumiko regretted.

"The cause that the body was exchanged is indistinct."

"That's right, but…"

"Let's live with this body for a while. "

"I understood. "

"Let's call the name of each other in accordance with the body. "

"I agreed, without consulting with the parents."

The chime of the entrance sounded.

"Yumiko! I'm home! Open the door!"

Suddenly two were surprised at the chime.

The grandfather who became Yumiko did it to open the door.

"What is it the foam that is in this room? "

It was the foam of a fire extinguisher, that it is in a room.

Two did not explain.

"I clean up. Does the grandfather eat the evening meal? "

Yes with Yumiko of the grandfather said.

Yumiko of the grandfather shut up.
